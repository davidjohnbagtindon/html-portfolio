The Best Manhwas According To Reiju

My Top 3 Manhwas Of All Time

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is set in a world where mysterious gates appear, releasing monsters that
threaten humanity, and only awakened humans called hunters can fight them. Among
them is Sung Jin-Woo, known as the weakest hunter, barely surviving low-level dungeons
and constantly risking his life just to earn a living.

After a near-death experience in a dangerous dungeon, Jin-Woo gains access to a unique
system that allows him to level up, gain skills, and grow stronger without limits. Unlike
other hunters whose strength is fixed, he continuously evolves, quickly surpassing
everyone around him through relentless battles and training.

As Jin-Woo’s power rises, he uncovers the hidden truths behind the gates, the system, and
the forces manipulating the world. His journey transforms him from humanity’s weakest
into its strongest protector, as he faces overwhelming enemies that threaten the fate of mankind.

Mercemary Enrollment

Mercenary Enrollment follows Yu Ijin, a boy who survives a plane crash and grows up as a
child soldier and elite mercenary. After ten years, he finally returns to South Korea and
reunites with his family, trying to live a normal life while hiding his violent past.

Enrolling in a regular high school, Ijin struggles to adjust to civilian life, especially school
politics, bullies, and social norms that feel more dangerous in a different way. Despite his
calm and quiet demeanor, his combat skills and instincts as a mercenary quickly surface
whenever his family or friends are threatened.

The story balances intense action with family and school drama, focusing on Ijin’s efforts
to protect the people he cares about while resisting a return to the life of violence he was
trained for.

Magic Emperor

Magic Emperor follows Zhuo Fan, a ruthless demonic cultivator who is betrayed and killed,
only to be reborn in the body of a weak servant boy. Retaining his past memories and
overwhelming experience, he begins a calculated rise in a brutal cultivation world where
power, status, and cunning determine survival.

Using intelligence, manipulation, and sheer ruthlessness rather than blind heroism,
Zhuo Fan strengthens his body, builds influence, and elevates the declining Luo clan
he serves. His methods are often cruel and morally gray, setting him apart from typical
righteous protagonists.

As Zhuo Fan grows stronger, he faces powerful sects and enemies from his past life, all
while struggling between his demonic nature and the unexpected loyalty and bonds he forms.
The story emphasizes strategy, dominance, and the cost of absolute power.
